This is exactly the behavior we have been warning about. A Prius approaches the stop-controlled intersection at Spring & 11th, **accelerates through** the stop sign without slowing, and continues at an estimated 50 mph on a residential block.

{{< youtube i2zL7MYdPCE >}}

## Why this matters

This is one driver, on one day. But it is not an outlier — it is a representative sample of the kind of driving Spring Street routinely sees. We have been [archiving this footage for years](https://youtube.com/@jazeee?si=ESJdzFSmUFvitEsS).

The problem is not going to fix itself. And once the [Bay Road Complete Streets project](/bay-road-project/) is built, the same driver — and many like them — will be routed onto our street even more often, because navigation apps will prefer it over the calmed Bay Road corridor.

## What we are doing about it

We are organizing for the City of Redwood City to fund **equivalent traffic calming** on Spring Street: bulb-outs, a mini-roundabout at Spring & 11th, and raised crosswalks.
