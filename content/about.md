## Who we are

We are residents of **Spring Street in North Fair Oaks** in San Mateo County, California. This site is a collective effort — no single author. We share a stop sign, a crosswalk, and a growing list of close calls.

## What we've witnessed

Years of speeding, stop-sign running, and near-misses with pedestrians and cyclists. We've been documenting these incidents on video — see [the playlist on @jazeee on YouTube](https://youtube.com/@jazeee?si=ESJdzFSmUFvitEsS) for the running record.

The most recent example: a Prius accelerating through our stop sign at an estimated 50 mph — [watch it here](https://youtu.be/i2zL7MYdPCE?si=44uelfMSrHpNO1EN).

## What we want

We support the City of Redwood City's investment in [calming Bay Road](https://share.google/Y9JDSyXXVB6sROs8a). We are not asking the city to cancel or slow that project. We are asking for the **complement**: equivalent calming on Spring Street to absorb the spillover that the Bay Road redesign will inevitably create.

The specific measures that work — and that we want studied for Spring — include:

- **Bulb-outs** at intersections to narrow the visual roadway and slow turns.
- **Mini-roundabouts** that physically constrain through-speeds.
- **Raised crosswalks and speed humps** at key crossings.
- **Stop-sign cameras or other enforcement** for chronic violators.
